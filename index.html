Your Coastal Sanctuary

Nestled in the picturesque village of Rooi-Els, Villa Flora offers an unparalleled escape where the majestic Kogelberg Mountains embrace the pristine coastline. This thoughtfully designed beach house combines modern luxury with the raw beauty of the Western Cape's most spectacular coastal setting.

Wake up to breathtaking ocean views, fall asleep to the gentle sound of waves, and spend your days exploring pristine beaches, dramatic clifftops, and world-class whale watching opportunities right at your doorstep.

Villa Flora isn't just accommodation – it's your gateway to experiencing the soul of South Africa's most beautiful coastal region.

Exceptional Amenities

Ocean Views

Wake up to panoramic ocean vistas from multiple rooms. Watch whales breach and dolphins play from your private balcony.

Mountain Backdrop

Surrounded by the dramatic Kogelberg Mountains, offering hiking trails and breathtaking sunset views.

Beach Access

Direct access to pristine beaches perfect for swimming, surfing, and beachcombing along the rugged coastline.

Luxury Comfort

Fully equipped modern kitchen, comfortable bedrooms, and a family orientated living area designed for relaxation.

Spectacular Sunrises

Experience magical dawns over the Indian Ocean, painting the sky in brilliant oranges and pinks.

Whale Watching

Prime location for seasonal whale watching, with Southern Right Whales frequenting the bay.
